ABOUT


HEXOTC allows for P2P, decentralized and zero-fee trading.

  • Buy and sell HEX over-the-counter, set your rate and get the best price!
  • Trade P2P to avoid market slippage via the HEXOTC smart-contract.
  • Make an offer, await a taker or cancel it anytime.
  • Check out any open offers and make your trade instantly.
  • Secure trustless escrow system - offers are held in the HEXOTC smart-contract until cancelled or taken.
  • Zero-fees, simply pay gas to mine your transaction on the Ethereum network.
  • Built for HEX, Powered by Ethereum.
